    # Bullfighter Rules 0.1.1

    1. Never leave the house without a camera
    2. Never leave the house without making your bed
    3. Never talk directly into the camera
    4. Never begin a sentence with “I, me, my”
    5. Start tomorrow, tonight
    6. Go to bed tired
    7. Dress appropriately and in a way that makes you feel confident
    8. Say no
    9. Say yes
    10. Show up early
    11. Smile
    12. Ask for help
    13. Say “I don’t know”
    14. Ask others about themselves
    15. Stay late
    16. Stay for dinner and drinks
    17. Hope for the future
    18. Own your process
    19. Show your work
    20. Never make excuses
    21. Complete one task at a time
    22. Delete all the apps from your phone except for texting/calling/contacts, navigation/transportation, and music
    23. Use your computer as a terminal
    24. Back up footage in three places - an internal drive, an external drive, and a cloud drive
    25. Reinvent yourself until you are the best in the world at what you do
    26. Be someone people want to work with
    27. Start projects people want to work on
    28. Sleep eight hours a night
    29. Wake up early every morning
    30. Think every morning
    31. Work out every day
    32. Read books every day
    33. Read classic and contemporary literature, books on history, architecture, sociology, psychology, religion, art, and design
    34. Go to used bookstores
    35. Go to local coffee shops
    36. Go to independent movie theatres
    37. Watch old movies
    38. Watch bad movies
    39. Watch with the director’s commentary
    40. Carry a notebook (or two)
    41. Keep a calendar
    42. Keep a to-do list
    43. Keep a journal
    44. Wear a uniform
    45. Keep an overnight bag in your car
    46. Minimize monthly expenses
    47. Make do without until you can afford the nice version of something
    48. When buying gifts, buy a nice version of a cheap thing rather than a cheap version of a nice thing
    49. Learn gun safety
    50. Learn to cook on a camping stove
    51. Learn to wash your clothes in the sink
    52. Learn to give stick and poke tattoos
    53. Learn card games
    54. Learn to change your mind
    55. Learn many skills
    56. Learn one craft
    57. Learn your tools
    58. Do things that scare you
    59. Tend a garden
    60. Travel as far as you can, as often as you can
    61. Learn how to find interesting things everywhere you go
    62. Learn to act graciously and appropriately around the very poor and very rich
    63. Give generously
    64. Love with abandon
    65. Speak concisely
    66. Never take credit
    67. Sometimes take blame
    68. Always take initiative
    69. Think slow
    70. Decide fast
    71. Don’t tell anyone what you’re going to do
    72. Show people what you’ve done
    73. Use your life all the way up
